A little about myself

What can I say about myself? I grew up in a small town in Waterville, Maine. After graduating high school, my family and I moved to Portland, also in Maine, and have lived there since. I live on the lake at my camp in Belgrade, Maine during the summer. I have a brother and two great parents.

Things I like to do

A few things I take interests in include; hiking with friends, playing basketball with friends and listening to music and just kicking back with people I'm close to. I also enjoy going out in the Old Port, which is the downtown area in Portland, for beer and food tasting. I recently started looking into stock trading and enjoy exploring new marketing ideas as well. In the summertime I work landscaping and actually enjoy it, and after a long days work I come home to boating and jet skiing on the lake.

I plan on living in the southern part of the state of Maine, and working out of that area as well. Business is in the future though, that is for certain!
